Linguistics Work

My work in Linguistics has centered mostly around Spanish. While studying for my M.A. Originally my interests centered around Traditional New Mexican Spanish, however, since I was in the south of the state, I decided to study Border Spanish instead. I wanted to find out more about the vowel deletion and weakening in Border Spanish. Later as I progressed in my Academic Career, I took an interest in Historical Linguistics and in Semantics. A few projects from that time can be found below.

Despite not being enrolled actively in any Ph.D. program, I am still studying and looking to learn about Linguistics as much as I can. Some future projects I plan on working on include the use of R/Python for sentiment analysis and hopefully work on Historic Romance Semantics.

In the meantime, however, I am teaching at NMSU as an Adjunct instructor.

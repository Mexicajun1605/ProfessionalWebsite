Language Learning Resources

The language learning resources below are some that I have used both to teach myself and others. I highly recommend the nature method, however, Grammar books will also be linked.

In the General section, I highly recommend starting off with Stephen Krashen's talk on how to learn languages. Languages cannot be learned like other skills. They must be lived.

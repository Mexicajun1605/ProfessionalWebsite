What is a personal Canon?

As defined by me, since this is my website, a personal canon is a list of works that you deem core to how you have developed as a person. These works span my career, personal, and spiritual life. I will be breaking them down into categories and then listing them in alphabetical order by title (Some do not have authors).

The reason why I am including such a thing on my website is so that you may know why I am the way I am and how I work the way I work. While I may not be able to quote all of these books to you immediately, they have indeed shaped who I am.
